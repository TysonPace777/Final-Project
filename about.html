About Us



Here at the Random Shopper, our team (Tyson Pace) wanted a way to gather ideas for shopping whenever someone felt like they just wanted to buy something but didn't know what. With this site the solution has been found! With one click of the button up to 6 products will be displayed from a random category along with their prices so you can gain ideas on what might peak your interest or gift ideas for others.
